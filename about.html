About Our School

History

Special Science Elementary School was established to provide quality science education to the children of Estancia, Iloilo. As a specialized public elementary school, we focus on developing scientific thinking from an early age. Our school is located within the Estancia Central Elementary School compound and serves Grades 1–6.

Mission & Vision

Mission:
To nurture scientifically curious, creative, and responsible learners through innovative, hands-on STEM experiences.
Vision:
To be the leading science elementary school in the region, producing future scientists, engineers, and innovators.

Our Values

  • 🔬 Curiosity
  • 🧠 Critical Thinking
  • 🤝 Collaboration
  • 🌱 Sustainability
  • ⭐ Excellence

Facilities

We are equipped with a modern science laboratory, computer room, library, and outdoor learning garden. Our teachers are trained in STEM education and regularly attend science workshops and seminars.
