Student Enrollment 2026-2027

Now Accepting Enrollees for SY 2026-2027

Grades 1 to 6 • Special Science Curriculum

Enrollment Period:
April 1 – May 31, 2026
School Year:
June 2026 – March 2027
Available Slots:
Limited to 30 students per grade level

Enrollment Requirements

For Incoming Grade 1

  • Birth Certificate (PSA Authenticated)
  • Barangay Clearance
  • Medical Certificate
  • 2 pcs 2x2 ID Picture
  • Parent/Guardian ID

For Grades 2–6 (Transferees)

  • Report Card (Form 138) from previous school
  • Birth Certificate (PSA)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Medical Certificate
  • 2 pcs 2x2 ID Picture

Enrollment Process

1

Submit Documents

Bring all required documents to the school office during enrollment period.

2

Entrance Assessment

Students will undergo a simple science aptitude and reading assessment.

3

Interview

Parent and child will have a short interview with the school head.

4

Payment & Confirmation

Pay the minimal school fees and receive the official enrollment confirmation.
