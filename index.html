Our Mission

To develop scientifically literate, creative, and responsible learners who are equipped to face the challenges of the 21st century through hands-on STEM education.

Our Vision

A premier science-oriented elementary school in Iloilo producing globally competitive young scientists and innovators.

Estancia, Iloilo

Located in the heart of Estancia, we proudly serve our community with quality science education since our establishment.

🧪

Hands-on Experiments

Weekly laboratory sessions where students explore chemistry, physics, and biology concepts.

🌍

Environmental Science

🤖

Robotics & Coding

Introducing young learners to programming and basic robotics using age-appropriate tools.
